Memory Rules

COOLERS is a competitive memory game !
The goal of the Memory is to find pairs.




This is the rule of the famous game Memory, also known as the Game of Pairs.

At the beginning of the game, shuffle all the cards and arrange them in rows, face down on the table.

Player 1 starts and turns over 2 cards, 2 possibilities:

If the cards are not identical (it's not a pair), he turns them over and then it's Player 2's turn to turn over 2 new cards.
On the other hand, if the 2 cards are identical, it's a pair! The player keeps the cards and has the right to play again...

When players have found all the pairs in the game, the game is over. The player with the most cards wins!
